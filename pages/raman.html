The 'MUST EAT' raman in New Chitose Aireport in Hokkaido (北海道新千歲機場)

Ebisoba Ichigen 一幻拉麵

It is not kidding the raman was recommanded as the 'must eat' in Hokkaido's airport due to its specia taste which was cooked by shrimp. Check the breif introduction from the airport's website!

Ichigen in Hokkaido's Airport

Where can I find it?

The restaurant is located in Xinyi District, near Shin Kong Mitsukoshi department store. This area is one of the most expensive areas in East Taipei. As a result, a nice meal experience is expected to eat in this restaurant.
Actually, this restaurant is so famous that you can always see people queueing in front of the door only for eating in.

What choices do I have?

Actually there is not much choice in this restaurant as this restaurant is so confident that you would surely love there shrimp raman. Thus, you almost can only find all kinds shrimp raman with different flavor of soup.
Additionally, some side dishes such as Gyoza or Onigiri could also be ordered but only with shrimp flavor too! ;p

How is the enviroment inside?

Needlss to say, the eating area in the restaurant is tidy and clean. You have enough space to eat comfortably there. There is also a bar area for single comers to eat relaxedly. It is quite a frienly environment to all kinds of visitors. The downside is, in order to provide comfortable seats to customers, there are onlt limited number of seats. As a result, you might need to queue outside of the restaurant if you do not book reservations in advance.

So How does it Taste?

To be honest, the only feeling I have after tasting this bowl of raman is -- 'SO SHRIMP'!!
This sounds like very useless opinion, but no kidding, the taste of shrimp is super strong. Thus whether this raman is delicious or not depends on how much you like the flavor of shrimp. Of course you can imagine this conclusion must come from the fact (without any boast) the retaurant is cooking the soup by a lot of shrimps.
The red part on the top of the noodle is also some kind of spice which is produced from shrimp too. This spice increase the different level of taste texture and brings another aroma of shrimp to the noodle.
All in all, this restaurant could be a good choice if you like seafood, especially shrimp. If not, then please find other restaurants suit you better.
